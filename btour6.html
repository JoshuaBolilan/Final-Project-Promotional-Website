Twin Lagoon, Coron



Description

Twin Lagoon in Coron, Palawan is a breathtaking natural attraction known for its crystal-clear waters surrounded by dramatic limestone cliffs. The lagoon is divided into two parts, connected by a small opening that you can swim through or cross during low tide.

This unique destination offers an unforgettable experience for those who love to swim, snorkel, or simply soak in the serene ambiance of nature. It's one of Coron's must-visit highlights.

Key Highlights:
  • Two tranquil lagoons separated by a limestone wall
  • Accessible by swimming under a rock arch or climbing a ladder
  • Perfect for swimming and snorkeling in calm, clear water
  • Surrounded by towering karst cliffs and marine biodiversity
