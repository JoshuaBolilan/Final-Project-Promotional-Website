Puerto Princesa Underground River, Palawan



Description

The Puerto Princesa Underground River is one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature, located in the beautiful province of Palawan, Philippines. It features a spectacular limestone karst landscape with an 8.2 km navigable underground river, making it one of the longest of its kind in the world.

Visitors can enjoy a boat tour through the subterranean cave system, marveling at unique rock formations, stalactites, and stalagmites, all while immersed in the tranquility of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Key Highlights:
  • Explore the 8.2 km underground river by paddle boat
  • See stunning rock formations and cave ecosystems
  • Part of the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park
  • Surrounded by rich biodiversity and pristine nature
