Sibaltan, Palawan



Description

Sibaltan, located in the northern part of El Nido, Palawan, is a tranquil fishing village known for its pristine beaches, vibrant marine life, and spectacular views. It remains less commercialized, providing a peaceful getaway from the crowds.

Sibaltan is an ideal spot for those looking to experience authentic local life, engage in eco-friendly tours, or simply unwind on secluded beaches. It's also known for its wonderful kayaking, snorkeling, and island-hopping experiences.

Key Highlights:
  • Peaceful village experience with picturesque views
  • Less crowded beaches perfect for relaxation
  • Snorkeling and island hopping to nearby islands
  • Rich cultural experience with local fishing and farming communities
