Honda Bay, Palawan



Description

Honda Bay, located in Puerto Princesa, Palawan, is a stunning collection of islands known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant coral reefs. It is one of the most popular spots for island hopping, snorkeling, and enjoying the natural beauty of the Philippines.

Visitors can explore several small islands, such as Cowrie Island, Starfish Island, and Luli Island, each offering unique experiences and beautiful surroundings.

Key Highlights:
  • Island hopping to popular islands such as Cowrie, Starfish, and Luli
  • Snorkeling in crystal-clear waters
  • White sandy beaches perfect for relaxation
  • Variety of marine life and vibrant coral reefs
