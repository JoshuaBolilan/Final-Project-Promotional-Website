Port Barton, Palawan



Description

Port Barton is a peaceful coastal village in San Vicente, Palawan, known for its laid-back atmosphere, beautiful beaches, and crystal-clear waters. It's a hidden gem offering a quiet alternative to the more commercialized areas of Palawan.

Whether you're snorkeling, island hopping, watching sea turtles, or simply relaxing by the beach, Port Barton offers a serene escape surrounded by nature.

Key Highlights:
  • Snorkeling with sea turtles and vibrant coral reefs
  • Island hopping to Paradise Island, German Island, and more
  • Relaxed atmosphere and fewer tourists
  • Scenic sunsets and beachfront dining
