Coron, Palawan



Description

Coron is a world-renowned destination in northern Palawan, Philippines, celebrated for its pristine lagoons, crystal-clear lakes, dramatic limestone cliffs, and vibrant marine life. It's a paradise for nature lovers, divers, and adventure seekers.

From the breathtaking Kayangan Lake to the mysterious Twin Lagoon and historic WWII shipwreck dive sites, Coron offers unforgettable experiences for every kind of traveler.

Key Highlights:
  • Kayangan Lake – the cleanest lake in the Philippines
  • Twin Lagoon – stunning twin bodies of water surrounded by karst cliffs
  • World War II Japanese shipwreck dive sites
  • Hot springs, island hopping, and white-sand beaches
