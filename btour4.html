Big Lagoon, El Nido



Description

Big Lagoon is one of the most iconic and stunning destinations in El Nido, Palawan. Surrounded by towering limestone cliffs and crystal-clear turquoise waters, it offers a paradise-like setting perfect for kayaking and sightseeing.

As you paddle through the narrow entrance into the lagoon, you're greeted by a dramatic view of calm waters, vibrant marine life, and lush greenery — making it a top choice for nature lovers and photographers alike.

Key Highlights:
  • Kayaking through calm, clear waters
  • Surreal limestone cliff surroundings
  • Great spot for drone and nature photography
  • Popular stop in El Nido’s Tour A island-hopping package
