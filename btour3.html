Kayangan Lake, Coron



Description

Often dubbed as the cleanest lake in the Philippines, Kayangan Lake is located in Coron, Palawan. Surrounded by towering limestone cliffs and crystal-clear waters, it offers an unforgettable experience for nature lovers and adventurers alike.

Accessible by a short hike, the lake is perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and taking in breathtaking views from the viewpoint overlooking the bay. Its mixture of fresh and saltwater also makes it a unique spot for exploring underwater rock formations.

Key Highlights:
  • Stunning viewpoint overlooking Coron Bay
  • Crystal-clear water ideal for swimming and snorkeling
  • Unique limestone formations and peaceful surroundings
  • Part of the protected ancestral domain of the Tagbanua people
