El Nido, Palawan



Description

El Nido is a breathtaking coastal town in the northern part of Palawan, Philippines, known for its crystal-clear waters, dramatic limestone cliffs, and vibrant marine life. It serves as the gateway to the Bacuit Archipelago, a paradise of hidden lagoons, white-sand beaches, and coral reefs.

Whether you're into island-hopping, snorkeling, diving, or simply soaking up the sun, El Nido offers an unforgettable experience surrounded by natural wonders.

Key Highlights:
  • Island hopping tours to Big Lagoon, Small Lagoon, and Secret Beach
  • Snorkeling and scuba diving in coral-rich waters
  • Kayaking through turquoise lagoons and coves
  • Stunning sunsets and vibrant local food scene
